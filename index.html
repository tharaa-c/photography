My story

Photography is my passion and my hobby, since the first day I got my little digital camera in 2008, I became attached to photograpy, tried so many styles so many themes. I feel so happy that I can connect with peopple through my lenses. My photographs ranges from Portraits, to nature, to people in action , kids, abstracts and many more.

 

Recently I am more interested in street photography and photojournalism. “Photography for me is not looking, it’s feeling. If you can’t feel what you’re looking at, then you’re never going to get others to feel anything when they look at your pictures."_ Don Mccollin
